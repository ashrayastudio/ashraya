Why Recall exists

Your brain is already carrying enough.

Families run on small details. Recall gives those details a safe place to land, so you can stop replaying them in your head.

Daily life

Restaurants, names, gift ideas, colors, repair notes, and the small recommendations people give you in passing.

“Where did we buy that lamp?”

Family care

Appointment notes, questions to ask later, routines, preferences, and the details one person usually has to remember for everyone.

“What did Mom say worked last time?”

Personal history

The places you went, the people you met, and the moments that are too ordinary for a photo album but still worth keeping.

“When did we visit that garden?”

How it works

Capture in seconds. Find in seconds.

Recall is designed for real life: quick entry when you are busy, plain-language search when you need the answer, and no noisy system to maintain.

Step 01

Add a memory

Type or speak a quick note. Add as much or as little context as you have.

Step 02

Let Recall organize it

Memories are grouped into simple categories like food, people, home, health, places, events, pets, and other.

Step 03

Ask naturally

Search the way you think. Recall brings back the detail without making you remember exact keywords.

Private by design

Your memories are not content for someone else.

Recall is being built with Ashraya Studio's privacy principles from the start: no ads, no selling data, and no account required to begin.

  • On-device processing wherever possible.
  • No ads on any tier.
  • No selling user data. Ever.
  • No account required to start using the app.
  • Plain-language privacy, not legal fog.

Fast enough to use

If capturing a memory takes too long, it will not happen. Recall is built for quick, low-friction entry.

Calm enough to trust

No streaks, no pressure, no productivity theater. The app should feel ready when you need it and quiet when you do not.

Useful before it is full

Even a handful of memories should be easy to browse, search, and retrieve.
